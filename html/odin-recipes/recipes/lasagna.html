Lasagna

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish consisting of bechamel sauce, ragu, pasta sheets and grated cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Minced meat
  • Butter roux
  • Milk and cream
  • Parmessan cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta sheets
  2. Cook bechamel
  3. Cook the ragu
  4. Layer it all together
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top
  6. Bake in the oven for 1h
